Coronavirus is a new type of virus that causes illness like common cold and Respiratory problems. This virus starts in Wuhan, China on the 31st of December 2019 and was treated as a common pneumonia with an unknown resource. More in depth research it turns out it is a new developed type of Virus.
The first death was reported on the 11th of January 2020 on Wuhan, China. WHO have declared a Global Health Emergency on the 30th January 2020 on Geneva an announced it to the press. The same call was held when the world deals with diseases such as Ebola, Zika and SARS.
And the first reported death outside China was from the Philippines on February 2nd. It is have been known that the virus have traveled from China to overseas and a lot of countries have issued a major lockdown and quarantine.
The virus was discovered to be here in Indonesia on March 2nd. On the 9th of April, with only a span of one month, the virus has spread across the 34th Province in Indonesia. The Government are slower in reaction than the other countries that have developed early lockdown, in fact the government of Indonesia started their ‘large-scale social restrictions’ movement on the March 16th, instead of implementing a nationwide lockdown. And this is also issued not from the main government itself but instead, from the regional government. And on May they applied the term of ‘New Normal’.
